Thoughts on CS 347

  • How would I design the course

    • I thought the information covered in the course was designed well, but personally I would have enjoyed a different in classroom approach. I felt like having a lab every single class with little information being taught in the class did not work for me. I feel like maybe have one day of more of a lecture style and then a larger lab the next class to put to work the information learned in the previous class. I felt that if I fell behing during one class/lab that I was discouraged from coming to the next class because we would be moving on and I would still have some gaps from the previous class.

  • What would I have the students do

    • Short, even open book reading quizzes I think would be really helpful to emphasize the most important concepts from a section.

  • What helped me learn and what would help others

    • I thought the textbook design and information was great whenever I felt stuck or lost most of the time I could easily find a solution to my problem in the textbook. I think continuing to use this textbook is a great idea.
